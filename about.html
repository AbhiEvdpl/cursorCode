Our Story

A Mission of Care & Excellence

Our mission is to deliver accurate, compassionate, and dependable long-term care pharmacy services to facilities across Idaho while improving resident outcomes and supporting nursing teams.

We founded our Idaho long-term care pharmacy with a clear purpose: to elevate medication safety, strengthen regulatory compliance, and enhance resident well-being in every community we serve.

"With decades of LTC experience, our team understands the unique challenges faced by Idaho's care facilities—particularly those in rural and frontier regions."

Locally Owned & Operated

Our Idaho Roots

Proudly Serving Our Home State

At Idaho Pharmacy, we believe the heart of excellent care begins right here at home. As a locally owned and operated long-term care pharmacy, we're proud to serve the communities we live in, raise our families in, and deeply care about.

Because we aren't part of a national chain or out-of-state corporation, there are no distant decision-makers, no delays, and no corporate barriers standing between your facility and the support you need.
