RecycleTroop Circular Electronics Partner Program

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1

Partner Status Definition

RecycleTroop Verified Partner status is granted to businesses that actively participate in circular electronics flows through RecycleTroop by:

  • a Selling broken mobile parts, and/or
  • b Purchasing used mobile devices
2

Badge Ownership & Usage Rights

  • The RecycleTroop Verified Partner badge remains the property of RecycleTroop.
  • Partners are granted a non-exclusive, revocable right to display the badge while the partnership is active.
3

Continuous Review

Partner status is reviewed continuously. RecycleTroop reserves the right to revoke partner status and badge usage at any time if cooperation ends or if misuse is detected.

4

No Financial Membership Fee

  • There is no subscription or marketing fee to be listed as a Verified Partner.
  • Optional technical badge installation is offered as a one-time service fee of 1,000 SEK.
5

Environmental Claims & Verification

The CO₂e figures shown in the badge represent verified internal calculations based on circular electronics trade via RecycleTroop. Figures are indicative and reflect real environmental contribution.

6

Termination of Status

If business cooperation with RecycleTroop ends, the partner must:

  • Remove the badge from all platforms
  • Will be removed from the Partner Page
7

Misuse

Unauthorized use, modification, or misleading use of the badge may result in immediate removal and potential legal action.

8

Badge Usage Rules

RecycleTroop Verified Partner Badge – Usage Rules

Approved Usage Areas

  • ✔ Company website
  • ✔ Web shop
  • ✔ Contact page
  • ✔ Footer
  • ✔ ESG / sustainability page
  • ✔ Marketing presentations
  • ✔ PDF documents & invoices (small version)

Not Allowed

  • ✘ On products as a product certification
  • ✘ In a way that suggests government or regulatory certification
  • ✘ On platforms unrelated to the partner's business
  • ✘ If active cooperation with RecycleTroop has ended

Size Rules

Placement Size
Website Header Min 48 px height – Max 80 px
Product Page / Footer 64–120 px
PDF Documents 24–40 px

No stretching, warping, or color distortion allowed

Placement Rules

Badge must:

  • ✔ Be clearly visible
  • ✔ Not be hidden behind popups
  • ✔ Not be placed near misleading claims
  • ✔ Always maintain original proportions

Allowed Versions

✔ Circular Badge
✔ Rectangular Trust Badge
✔ Rectangular CO₂e Impact Badge
✔ Light & Dark Mode versions

If you have questions about these terms, please contact us.
