Our Onboarding Process

Five Steps to Seamless Service

We've refined our onboarding process to ensure a smooth transition for your facility, staff, and residents.

1

Initial Consultation

We meet with administrators, DONs, and clinical leads to understand your facility's unique needs:

  • Facility type and census
  • Packaging preferences
  • Delivery needs
  • eMAR/EHR systems
  • Medication workflows
2

Facility Assessment & Customization

We develop a tailored pharmacy service plan covering:

  • Packaging format
  • Delivery schedule
  • Training requirements
  • After-hours protocol
3

eMAR/EHR Setup & Integration

Our team assists with complete system integration:

  • Full integration setup
  • Medication import
  • Staff access setup
  • Ongoing technical guidance
4

Nursing & Staff Training

We offer hands-on and virtual training for your team:

  • Medication pass procedures
  • Packaging handling
  • eMAR functionality
  • Emergency ordering protocol
5

Go-Live & Quality Follow-Up

During your first 30 days, we provide dedicated support:

  • On-site or virtual support
  • Audit checks
  • Accuracy & workflow review
  • Prompt issue resolution
