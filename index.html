Your Idaho Long-Term Care Pharmacy Partner

Welcome to Idaho Pharmacy, LLC. — Idaho's Trusted Long-Term Care Pharmacy Partner.

Idaho Pharmacy is a dedicated, independent, locally owned and operated, Idaho long-term care (LTC) pharmacy, serving skilled nursing facilities, assisted living communities, memory care, group homes, behavioral health programs, home health, hospice, and other long-term care providers across the state.

We specialize exclusively in LTC pharmacy services—no retail distractions, no walk-ins—so your residents and staff receive fast, accurate, and dependable medication support backed by Idaho-based expertise.
