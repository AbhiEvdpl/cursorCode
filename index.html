Why Distributors & OEMs Choose American Hose & Gasket

We combine wholesale pricing, reliable product quality, and responsive service to help you stay competitive in a fast-moving marketplace.

Proven Product Quality

We partner with ISO-certified factories and maintain a dedicated office in Asia that audits production and quality standards. Products are checked on-site before shipping to help ensure consistent, reliable performance.

Fast, Reliable Shipping

Most standard orders can ship the same day from our Decatur, AL warehouse, with additional support from Louisville, KY. You'll be advised quickly if a special request impacts lead time.

Freight Advantages

We ship FOB Decatur, AL, and offer free freight on qualifying large orders—helping distributors protect margin while staying competitive on delivered costs.

1-Year Product Warranty

Our products are warranted to be free from defects under normal use for one year. Our liability is limited to the cost of the proven defective product, and claims are handled through a straightforward written authorization and return process.
